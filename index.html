An Explainable Vision-Language Model Framework for Lumbar Spinal Stenosis Diagnosis
Md Sajeebul Islam Sk, Md Mehedi Hasan Shawon, Md. Golam Rabiul Alam, Ph.D.
*In review, 2026 / arXiv

Reducing the difficulty of accurate and explainable Lumbar Spinal Stenosis diagnosis from MRI by proposing an end-to-end explainable VLM framework using Spatial Patch Cross-Attention, Adaptive PID-Tversky loss, and automated radiology report generation module.

FoundationalECGNet: A Lightweight Foundational Model for ECG-based Multitask Cardiac Analysis
Md Sajeebul Islam Sk, Md Jobayer, Md Mehedi Hasan Shawon, Md. Golam Rabiul Alam, Ph.D.
*In review, 2026 / arXiv

Propose a foundation model that reduces noise, biased prediction, overfitting, poor generalization, and model size by using Morlet–DB4 wavelet denoising, ADASYN class balancing, pretraining CBAM–GAT–TST attention architecture and predicts Normal/Abnormal ECGs and five cardiac diseases from ECG diverse datasets.

Unveiling personality traits through Bangla speech using Morlet wavelet transformation and BiG
Md Sajeebul Islam Sk, Md. Golam Rabiul Alam, Ph.D.
Natural Language Processing Journal, 2024
paper

Create Bengali language based speech datasets and a speech-text based personality classification framework by introducing MoMF feature extraction, and developing DistilRo (DistilBERT and RoBERTa) and BiG (Bi-LSTM and GRU) soft-voting ensemble models to predicts ten different personality traits.

Identification of Drug-addicted People using Short Length of Voice Signal through Haar and Symlet Wavelet Transform
Sadia Afrin Md Sajeebul Islam Sk, Md. Kazi Nazmul Islam Dr. Md. Rafiqul Islam
IJRSI, 2025
paper

Analyze 5-second speech signals with Haar, Sym2, db4 wavelets and calculated PSNR, and SNR values for both drug-addicted and non-addicted people.
